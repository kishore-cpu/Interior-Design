WORKFLOW

Our Process

A systematic, transparent process designed to take your interior dream from concept to reality hassle-free.

1

Consultation & Requirement

Initial meeting to understand your taste, functionality needs, style preferences, and overall budget.

2

Concept & 3D Design

Detailed floor plans, layout selections, and realistic 3D visualizations showing your space before execution.

3

Material Selection

Choosing laminates, hardware, textures, stones, and lighting fixtures together to match the design style.

4

Execution & Installation

On-site carpentry, civil modification, false ceiling assembly, electrical, plumbing, painting, and fitting.

5

Final Handover

Thorough cleaning, final site inspection, quality control checks, and handover of your pristine dream home.
